Hello! My name is Sofia Vidallet and I am from Corrientes, Argentina. I work as a music teacher at a secondary school. In my free time, I enjoy playing the piano and going for walks along the beautiful "costanera" (riverfront) in my city.

Corrientes, Argentina

Corrientes is a beautiful province in northeastern Argentina. It is famous for the Iberá Wetlands, its rich history, and chamamé music. The weather is warm, and it is a wonderful place to enjoy nature and outdoor adventures.
